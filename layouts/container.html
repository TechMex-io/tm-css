Layouts - Container

Three widths are available:
1040px
1200px
1350px 

<div data-container="width:1040">
  <p>Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet consectetur adipisicing elit. Quae, quod.</p>
</div>

Custom widths can be declared by overriding the --container-size property of the [data-container] element. 

        <div data-container style="--container-size: 76.6875rem">
          <p>Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet consectetur adipisicing elit. Quae, quod.</p>
        </div>
