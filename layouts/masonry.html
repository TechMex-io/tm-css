Demo:

Code:

      <div data-masonry="col-size:sm gap:sm">
        <figure>
          <img src="https://picsum.photos/200/300" alt="">
        </figure>
        <figure>
          <img src="https://picsum.photos/600/300" alt="">
        </figure>
        <figure>
          <img src="https://picsum.photos/400/300" alt="">
        </figure>
      </div>

API:

col-size:[sm, md, lg]
gap:[sm, md, lg]
num-cols:[2, 3, 4, 5, 6]

note: `num-cols` will override `col-size` but is not responsive.
