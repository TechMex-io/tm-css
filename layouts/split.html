Layouts - Split

Demo:

Code:

      <form data-split="3/4 gap:1 collapse:sm align:end">
        <label for="email" data-stack="gap:1">
          Email Adress
          <input type="text" name="email" id="email" />
        </label>
        <button>Subscribe</button>
      </form>

Edit:

API:

[1/4, 1/3, 3/4, 2/3]
collapse:[sm, md, lg]
align:[start, end, between, around]
gap:[1, 2, 3]
